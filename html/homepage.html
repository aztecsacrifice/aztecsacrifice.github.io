Below is the xiuhp≈çhualli, one of the two primary calendars used by the Aztecs.


The goal of this page is to allow the user to point and click on certain rings of the calendar.
The rings show the cyclical nature of the ritual practice of the Aztecs; aka, each glyph corresponds with a day in a month in the Aztec time system.
The symbols used aren't directly correlated with particular rituals, though the cyclical nature of it provides a good visual for a basis of keeping track of them.
Overall, there will be about ~18 links to different pages -- 1 page per ritual.
