Nemontemi

This is not considered a month, nor is there any ritual involved; in fact, the whole point of nemontemi is to do nothing

These are the five remaining days in which the Aztec calendar could not prescribe a month to.

Anyone who was born on these days had no day sign, and therefore no meaning. They were given the name "Nemo" and was cursed to live in poverty upon earth

As a result, it the Aztecs often feared these days and made a point out of doing nothing.
